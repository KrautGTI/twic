There's the Battle of Burgoyne
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
There's the Battle of Burgoyne -
Over, every Day,
By the Time that Man and Beast
Put their work away -
"Sunset" sounds majestic -
But that solemn War
Could you comprehend it
You would chastened stare -
