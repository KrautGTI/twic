Did the harebell loose her girdle
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Did the Harebell loose her girdle
To the lover Bee
Would the Bee the Harebell
Much as formerly?
Did the "Paradise" - -
Yield her moat of pearl -
