We play at paste
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
We play at Paste -
Till qualified, for Pearl -
Then, drop the Paste -
And deem ourself a fool -
The Shapes - though - were similar -
And our new Hands
Learned -tactics -
Practising -
