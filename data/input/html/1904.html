Dreams are the subtle dower
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Dreams are the subtle Dower
That make us rich an Hour -
Then fling us poor
Out of the Purple Door
Into the Precinct raw
Possessed before -
