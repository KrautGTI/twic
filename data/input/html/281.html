Is it true, dear Sue?
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Is it true, dear Sue?
Are there
I should'nt like to come
For fear of joggling Him!
If you could shut him up
In a Coffee Cup,
Or tie him to a pin
Till I got in -
Or make him fast
To "Toby's" fist -
Hist! Whist! I'd come!
