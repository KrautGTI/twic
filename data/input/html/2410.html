A lane of yellow led the eye
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A lane of Yellow led the eye
Unto a Purple Wood
Whose soft inhabitants to be
Surpasses solitude
If Bird the silence contradict
Or flower presume to
In that low summer of the West
Impossible to know -
