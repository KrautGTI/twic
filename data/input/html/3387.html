Absent Place -- an April Day --
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
Absent Place -- an April Day --
Daffodils a -- blow
Homesick curiosity
To the Souls that snow --
Drift may block within it
Deeper than without --
Daffodil delight but
Him it duplicate --
4.To the] Unto
