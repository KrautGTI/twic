We shall find the cube of the rainbow
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
We shall find the Cube of the Rainbow -
Of that - there is no doubt -
But the Arc of a Lover's conjecture
Eludes the finding out -
