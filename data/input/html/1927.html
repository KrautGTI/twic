"Tomorrow" whose location
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
"Tomorrow" - whose location
The Wise deceives
Though it's hallucination
Is last that leaves -
Tomorrow, thou Retriever
Of every tare -
Of Alibi art thou
Or ownest where?
