Opon Concluded Lives
There's nothing cooler falls -
Than Life's sweet Calculations -
The mixing Bells and Palls -
Makes Lacerating Tune -
To Ears the Dying side -
'Tis Coronal - and Funeral -
Saluting - in the Road -
