The most important population
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The most important population
Unnoticed dwell.
They have a heaven each instant
Not any hell.
Their names, unless you know them,
'Twere useless tell.
Of and other nations
The grass is full.
