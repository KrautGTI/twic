We play at paste
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
We play at Paste -
Till qualified for Pearl -
Then, drop the Paste -
And deem Ourself a fool -
The Shapes, tho', were similar,
And our new Hands
Learned Gem Tactics
