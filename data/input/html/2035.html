Those not live yet
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Those not live yet
Who doubt to live again -
"Again" is of a twice
But this - is one -
The Ship beneath the Draw
Aground - is he?
Death - so - the Hyphen of the Sea -
Deep is the Schedule
Of the Disk to be -
Costumeless Consciousness,
That is he -
