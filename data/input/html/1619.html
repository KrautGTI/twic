The pungent atom in the air
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The pungent Atom in the Air
Admits of no debate -
All that is named of Days
Relinquished our Estate -
For what Department of Delight
As positive are we
As Limit of Dominion
Or Dams - of Extasy -
