Water is taught by thirst;
Poems by Emily Dickinson: Third Series, Todd, 1896
Water is taught by thirst;
Land, by the oceans passed;
Transport, by throe;
Peace, by its battles told;
Love, by memorial mould;
Birds, by the snow.
