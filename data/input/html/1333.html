Spring comes on the world
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Spring comes on the World -
I sight the Aprils -
Hueless to me, until thou come
As, till the Bee
Blossoms stand negative,
Touched to Conditions
By a Hum -
