To be alive is power
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
To be alive - is Power -
Existence - in itself -
Without a further function -
Omnipotence - Enough -
To be alive - and will! -
'Tis able as a God -
The Maker - of Ourselves - be what -
Such being Finitude!
