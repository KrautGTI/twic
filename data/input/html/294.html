Jesus! thy crucifix
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Jesus! thy Crucifix
Enable thee to guess
The smaller size -
Jesus! thy second face
Mind thee - in Paradise -
Of Our's.
