Art thou the thing I wanted?
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
Art thou the thing I wanted?
Begone -- my Tooth has grown --
Supply the minor P alate
That has not starved so long --
I tell thee while I waited
The mystery of Food
Increased till I abjured it
