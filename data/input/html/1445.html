The leaves like women interchange
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The Leaves like Women, interchange
Sagacious Confidence -
Somewhat of Nods and somewhat
Portentous inference -
The Parties in both cases
Enjoining secrecy -
Inviolable compact
To notoriety.
