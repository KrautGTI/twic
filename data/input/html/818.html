When I hoped I feared
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
When I hoped, I feared -
Since I hoped I dared
Everywhere alone
As a church remain -
Spectre cannot harm
Serpent cannot charm
He is Prince of Harm
Who hath suffered him -
