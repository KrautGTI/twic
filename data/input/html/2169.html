Come show thy Durham breast
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Come show thy Durham Breast
To her who loves thee best
Delicious Robin -
And if it be not me
At least within my Tree
Do the avowing -
Thy Nuptial so minute
Perhaps is more astute
Than vaster suing -
For so to soar away
Is our propensity
The Day ensuing -
