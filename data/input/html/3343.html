The Poets light but Lamps --
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
The Poets light but Lamps --
Themselves -- go out --
The Wicks they stimulate --
If vital Light
Inhere as do the Suns --
Each Age a Lens
Disseminating their
Circumference --
