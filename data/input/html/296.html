Teach him when he makes the names
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Teach Him - when He makes the -
Such an one - to say -
On his babbling - Berry - lips -
As should sound - to me -
Were my Ear - as near his nest -
As my - today -
As should sound -
"Forbid us not" -
Some like "Emily."
