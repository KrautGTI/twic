None can experience stint
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
None can experience stint
Who Bounty -- have not known --
The fact of Famine -- could not be
Except for Fact of Corn --
Want -- is a meagre Art
Acquired by Reverse --
The Poverty that was not Wealth --
Cannot be Indigence
