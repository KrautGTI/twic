An ignorance a sunset
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
An ignorance a Sunset
Confer opon the Eye -
Of Territory - Color -
Circumference - Decay -
It's Amber Revelation
Exhilirate - Debase -
Omnipotence' inspection
Of Our inferior face -
And when the solemn features
Confirm - in Victory -
We start - as if detected
In Immortality -
