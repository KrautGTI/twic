For largest woman's heart I knew
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
For largest Woman's Heart I knew -
'Tis little I can do -
And yet the largest Woman's Heart
Could hold an Arrow - too -
And so, instructed by my own,
I tenderer, turn me to.
