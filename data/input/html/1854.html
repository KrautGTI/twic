A rat surrendered here
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A Rat surrendered here
A brief career of Cheer
And Fraud and Fear.
Of Ignominy's due
Let all addicted to
Beware -
The most obliging Trap
It's tendency to snap
Cannot resist -
Temptation is the Friend
Repugnantly resigned
At last.
