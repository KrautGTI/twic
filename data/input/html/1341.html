Falsehood of thee could I suppose
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Falsehood of Thee, could I suppose
'Twould undermine the Sill
To which my Faith pinned Block by Block
Her Cedar Citadel -
