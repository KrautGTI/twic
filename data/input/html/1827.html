You cannot take itself
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
You Cannot take itself
From any Human soul -
That indestructible estate
Enable him to dwell -
Impregnable as Light
That every man behold
But take away as difficult
As undiscovered Gold -
