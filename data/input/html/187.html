Safe in their alabaster chambers
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Deep in their alabaster chambers,
Untouched by morn and untouched by noon,
Sleep the mute members of the Resurrection
Roof of satin and rafter of stone,
