Abraham to kill him
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Abraham to kill him
Was distinctly told -
Isaac was an Urchin -
Abraham was old -
Not a hesitation -
Abraham complied -
Flattered by Obeisance
demurred -
Isaac - to his Children
Lived to tell the tale -
Moral - with a Mastiff
Manners may prevail.
