At last to be identified!
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
At last, to be identified!
At last, the lamps opon thy side
The rest of Life
Past Midnight! Past the Morning Star!
Past Sunrise!
Ah, What leagues there
Between our feet, and Day!
