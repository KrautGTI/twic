I cannot be ashamed
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
I cannot be ashamed
Because I cannot see
The love you offer -
Magnitude
Reverses Modesty
And I cannot be proud
Because a Hight so high
Involves Alpine
Requirements
And services of Snow -
