South winds jostle them
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
South Winds jostle them -
Bumblebees come
Hover - Hesitate - Drink - and are gone -
Butterflies pause - on their passage Cashmere -
I, softly plucking,
Present them - Here -
