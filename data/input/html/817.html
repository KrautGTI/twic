When I hoped I feared
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
When I hoped - I feared -
Since - I hoped - I dared
Everywhere - alone -
As a church - remain -
Ghost - may not alarm -
Serpent - may not charm -
He is King of Harm -
Who hath suffered Him -
