Death warrants are supposed to be
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Death warrants are supposed to be
An enginery of Equity
A merciful mistake
A pencil in an Idol's Hand
A Devotee has oft consigned
To Crucifix or Block
