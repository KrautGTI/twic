All the letters I could write
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
All the letters I could write,
Were not fair as this -
Syllables of Velvet -
Sentences of Plush -
Depths of Ruby, undrained -
Hid, Lip, for Thee,
Play it were a Humming Bird
And sipped just Me -
