Truth is as old as God
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Truth - is as old as God -
His Twin identity
And will endure as long as He
A Co-Eternity -
And perish on the Day
Himself is borne away
From Mansion of the Universe
A lifeless Deity.
