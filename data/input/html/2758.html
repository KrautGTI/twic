Alone, I cannot be --
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
Alone, I cannot be --
The Hosts -- do visit me --
Recordless Company --
Who baffle Key --
They have no Robes, nor Names --
No Almanacs -- nor Climes --
But general Homes
Like Gnomes --
Their Coming, may be known
By Couriers within --
Their going -- is not --
For they're never gone --
