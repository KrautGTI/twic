He was my host - he was my guest
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
He was my host - he was my guest,
I never to this day
If I invited him could tell,
Or he invited me.
So infinite our intercourse
So intimate, indeed,
Analysis as capsule seemed
To keeper of the seed.
