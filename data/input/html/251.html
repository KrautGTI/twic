Ah, necromancy sweet!
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Ah, Sweet!
Ah, Wizard erudite!
Teach me the skill,
That I the pain
Surgeons assuage in vain,
Nor Herb of all the plain
Can heal!
