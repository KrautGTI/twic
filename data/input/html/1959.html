To the stanch dust
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
To the stanch dust
We safe commit thee.
Tongue, if it hath,
Inviolate to thee -
Breezes adjoin, and sanctity enforce thee -
Passenger of infinity!
