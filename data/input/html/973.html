A first mute coming
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A first Mute Coming -
In the Stranger's House -
A first fair Going -
When the Bells rejoice -
A first Exchange - of
What hath mingled - been -
For Lot - exhibited to
Faith - alone -
