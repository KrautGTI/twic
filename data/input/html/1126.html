Unfulfilled to observation
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Unfulfilled to Observation -
Incomplete - to Eye -
But to Faith - a Revolution
In Locality -
Unto Us - the Suns extinguish -
To our Opposite -
New Horizons - they embellish -
Fronting Us - with Night.
