Somewhat to hope for
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Somewhat, to hope for,
Be it ne'er so far
Is Capital against Despair -
Somewhat, to suffer,
Be it ne'er so keen -
If terminable, may be borne -
