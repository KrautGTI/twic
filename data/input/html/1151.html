Time feels so vast that were it not
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Time feels so vast that were it not
For an Eternity -
I fear me this Circumference
Engross my Finity -
To His exclusion, who prepare
By Processes of Size
For the Stupendous Vision
Of His Diameters -
