'Tis easier to pity those when dead
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
easier to pity those when dead
That which pity previous
Would have saved
A Tragedy enacted
Secures applause
That Tragedy enacting
Too seldom does
