Bind me I still can sing
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Bind me - I still can sing -
Banish - my mandolin
Strikes true, within -
Slay - and my Soul shall rise
Chanting to Paradise -
Still thine -
