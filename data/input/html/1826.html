What tenements of clover
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
What tenements of Clover
Are fitting for the Bee
What edifices azure
For Butterflies and me
What residences nimble
Arise and evanesce
Without a rhythmic rumor
Or an assaulting guess.
