Meeting by accident
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Meeting by Accident,
We hovered by design -
As often as a Century
An error so divine
Is ratified by Destiny,
But Destiny is old
And economical of Bliss
As Midas is of Gold -
