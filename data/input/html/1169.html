It is a lonesome glee
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
It is a lonesome Glee -
Yet sanctifies the Mind -
With fair association -
Afar opon the Wind
A Bird to overhear -
Delight without a Cause -
Arrestless as invisible -
A Matter of the Skies.
