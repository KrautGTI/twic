The road to Paradise is plain
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The Road to Paradise is plain
And holds scarce one -
Not that it has not room
But we presume
A florid road
Is more preferred -
The Guests of Paradise are few -
Not me, nor you -
But unsuspected things -
Mines have no Wings -
