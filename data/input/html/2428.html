One crown that no one seeks
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
One crown that no one seeks
And yet the highest head
isolation coveted
stigma deified
While Pontius Pilate lives
In whatsoever hell
That coronation pierces him
He recollects it well.
