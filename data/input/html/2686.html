[Sh]should you but fail [at] -- Sea --
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
[Sh]should you but fail [at] -- Sea --
[In] sight of me --
[Or] doomed lie --
[Ne]xt Sun -- to die --
[O]r rap -- at Paradise -- unheard
I'd harass God
Until he let [you] in!
