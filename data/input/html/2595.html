Water, is taught by thirst.
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
Water, is taught by thirst.
Land -- by the Oceans passed.
Transport -- by throe --
Peace -- by it's battles told --
Love, by Memorial Mold --
Birds, by the Snow.
