All I may if small
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
All I may, if small,
Do it not display
Larger for the Totalness -
'Tis Economy
To bestow a World
And withold a Star -
Utmost, is Munificence -
Less, tho' larger, poor -
