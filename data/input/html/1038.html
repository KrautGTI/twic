Autumn overlooked my knitting
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Autumn - overlooked my Knitting -
Dyes - said He - have I -
Could disparage a Flamingo -
Show Me them - said I -
Cochineal - I chose - for deeming
It resemble Thee -
And the little Border - Dusker -
For resembling Me -
