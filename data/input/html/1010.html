So much summer
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
So much Summer
Me for showing
Illegitimate -
Would a Smile's minute bestowing
Too exorbitant
To the Lady
With the Guinea
Look - if she should know
Crumb of Mine
A Robin's Larder
Would suffice to stow -
