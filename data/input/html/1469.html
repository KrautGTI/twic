A shade opon the mind there passes
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A Shade opon the mind there passes
As when on Noon
A Cloud the mighty Sun encloses
Remembering
That some there be too numb to notice
Oh God
Why give if Thou must take away
The Loved?
