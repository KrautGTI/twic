'Twould ease a butterfly
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
'Twould ease - a Butterfly -
Elate - a Bee -
Thou'rt neither -
Neither - thy capacity -
But, Blossom, were I,
I would rather be
Thy moment
Than a Bee's Eternity -
Content of fading
Is enough for me -
Fade I unto Divinity -
And Dying - Lifetime -
Ample as the Eye -
Her least attention raise on me -
