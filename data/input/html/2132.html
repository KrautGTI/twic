His little hearse like figure
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
His little Hearse like Figure
Unto itself a Dirge
To a delusive Lilac
The vanity divulge
Of industry and ethics
And every founded thing
For the divine Perdition
Of idleness and spring -
