Image of light, adieu
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Image of Light, Adieu -
Thanks for the interview -
So long - so short -
Preceptor of the whole -
Coeval Cardinal -
Impart - Depart -
