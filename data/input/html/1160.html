Expectation is contentment
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Expectation - is Contentment -
Gain - Satiety -
But Satiety - Conviction
Of Nescessity
Of an Austere trait in Pleasure -
Good, without alarm
Is a too established Fortune -
Danger - deepens Sum -
