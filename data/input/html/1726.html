Yesterday is history
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Yesterday is History,
'Tis so far away -
Yesterday is Poetry - 'tis Philosophy -
Yesterday is mystery -
Where it is Today
While we shrewdly speculate
Flutter both away
