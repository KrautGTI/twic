Experiment to me
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Experiment to Me
Is Every One I meet
If It contain a Kernel -
The figure of a Nut
Presents opon a Tree
Equally plausibly -
But Meat within is requisite
To Squirrels, and to Me -
