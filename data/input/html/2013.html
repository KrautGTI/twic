To mend each tattered faith
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
To mend each tattered Faith
There is a needle fair
Though no indicate -
'Tis threaded in the Air -
And though it do not wear
As if it never Tore
'Tis very indeed
And specious as before -
