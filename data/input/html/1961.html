To the unwilling dust
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
To the stanch Dust
We safe commit thee -
Tongue if it hath,
Inviolate to thee -
Silence - denote -
And Sanctity - enforce thee -
Passenger - of Infinity -
