All the letters I can write
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
All the letters I can write
Are not fair as this -
Syllables of Velvet -
Sentences of Plush,
Depths of Ruby, undrained,
Hid, Lip, for Thee -
Play it were a Humming Bird -
And just sipped - me -
