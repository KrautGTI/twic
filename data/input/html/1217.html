Finding is the first act
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Finding is the first Act
The second, loss,
Third, Expedition for the "Golden Fleece"
Fourth, no Discovery -
Fifth, no Crew -
Finally, no Golden Fleece -
Jason, sham, too -
