The harm of years is on him
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The harm of Years is on him -
The infamy of Time -
Depose him like a Fashion
And give Dominion room -
Forget his Morning Forces -
The Glory of Decay
Is a minuter Pageant
Than least Vitality.
