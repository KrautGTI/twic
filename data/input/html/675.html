Presentiment is that long shadow on the lawn
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Presentiment - is that long shadow - on the Lawn -
Indicative that Suns go down -
The notice to the startled Grass
That Darkness - is about to pass -
