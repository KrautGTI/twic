Alter! When the hills do
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Alter! When the Hills do -
Falter! When the Sun
Question if His Glory
Be the Perfect One -
Surfeit! When the Daffodil
Doth of the Dew -
Even as Herself - Sir -
I will - Of You -
