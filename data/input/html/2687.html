Teach Him -- when He makes the names --
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
Teach Him -- when He makes the names --
Such an one -- to say --
On his babbling -- Berry -- lips --
As should sound -- to me --
Were my Ear -- as near his nest --
As my thought -- today --
As should sound --
"Forbid us not" --
Some like "Emily."
