I reason, earth is short
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
I reason -
Earth is short -
And Anguish - absolute -
And many - hurt -
But,
I reason -
We should -
The - Vitality
Could not -
But
I reason -
That in "Heaven" -
- it will be -
Some - given -
But -
