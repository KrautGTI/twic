I reason, earth is short
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
I reason -
Earth is short -
And Anguish - absolute -
And many - hurt -
But, What of that?
I reason -
We should die -
The best - Vitality
Could not excel Decay -
But What of that?
I reason -
That in "Heaven" -
Somehow - it will be even -
Some new Equation - given -
But - What of that!
