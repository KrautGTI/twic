Absent place an April day
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Absent Place - an April Day -
Daffodils a'blow
Homesick curiosity
To the Souls that snow -
Drift may block within it
Deeper than without -
Daffodil delight but
Him it duplicate -
