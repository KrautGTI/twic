Belshazzar had a letter
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
had a Letter
He never had but one

Concluded and begun
In that immortal Copy
The Conscience of us all
Can read without it's glasses
On Revelation's Wall
