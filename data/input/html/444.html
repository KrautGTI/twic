It's like the light
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
It's like the Light -
A fashionless Delight -
It's like the Bee -
A dateless - Melody -
It's like the Woods -
Private - Like the Breeze -
Phraseless - yet it stirs
The proudest Trees -
It's like the morning -
Best - when it's done -
And the Everlasting Clocks -
Chime - Noon!
