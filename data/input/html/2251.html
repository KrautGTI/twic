The heart has many doors
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The Heart has many Doors -
I can but knock -
For any sweet "Come in"
Impelled to hark -
Not saddened by repulse,
Repast to me
That somewhere, there exists,
Supremacy -
