One and One -- are One --
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
One and One -- are One --
Two -- be finished using --
Well enough for Schools --
But for Minor Choosing --
Life -- just -- Or Death --
Or the Everlasting --
More -- would be too vast
For the Soul's Comprising --
