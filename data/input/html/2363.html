Speech is one symptom of affection
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Speech is one symptom of affection
And Silence one -
The perfectest communication
Is heard of none
Exists and it's indorsement
Is had within -
Behold said the Apostle
Yet had not seen!
