Left in immortal youth
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Left in immortal Youth
On that low Plain
That hath nor Retrospection
Nor Again -
Ransomed from years -
Sequestered from Decay
Canceled like Dawn
In comprehensive Day -
