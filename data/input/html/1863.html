The heart is the capital of the mind
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The Heart is the Capital of the Mind -
The Mind is a single State -
The Heart and the Mind together make
A single Continent.
One - is the population -
Numerous enough -
This ecstatic Nation -
Seek - it is Yourself -
