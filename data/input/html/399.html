Should you but fail at sea
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Should you but fail at - Sea -
In sight of me -
Or doomed lie -
Next Sun - to die -
Or rap - at Paradise - unheard -
I'd -
Until He let you in!
