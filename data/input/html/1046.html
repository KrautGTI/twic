So the eyes accost and sunder
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
So the Eyes accost - and sunder
In an Audience -
Stamped - occasionally - forever -
So may Countenance
Entertain - without addressing
Countenance of One
In a Neighboring Horizon -
Gone - as soon as known -
