As if the sea should part
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
As if the Sea should part
And show a further Sea -
And that - a further - and the Three
But a Presumption be -
Of Periods of Seas -
Unvisited of Shores -
Themselves the Verge of Seas to be -
Eternity - is Those -
