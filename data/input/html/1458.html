Of the heart that goes in and closes the door
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Of the Heart that goes in, and closes the Door
Shall the Playfellow Heart complain
Though the Ring is unwhole, and the
Can never be fitted again?
