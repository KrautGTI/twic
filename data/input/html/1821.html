Pink - small - and punctual
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Pink - small - and punctual -
Aromatic - low -
Covert in April -
Candid - in May -
Dear to the Moss -
Known of the Knoll -
Next to the Robin
In every Human Soul -
Bold little Beauty -
Bedecked with thee
Nature forswears
Antiquity -
