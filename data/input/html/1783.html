Whether they have forgotten
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Whether they have forgotten
Or are forgetting now
Or never remembered -
Safer not to know -
Miseries of conjecture
Are a softer wo
Than a Fact of Iron
Hardened with I know -
