To the unwilling dust
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
To the unwilling dust
We soft commit thee.
Guile if it hath,
Inviolate to thee -
Breezes caress and firmament salute thee,
Nothing affront thy secrecy.
