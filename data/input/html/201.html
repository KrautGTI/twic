Mute thy coronation
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Mute - thy coronation -
Meek - my Vive le roi,
Fold a tiny courtier
In thine ermine, Sir,
There to rest revering
Till the pageant by,
I can murmur broken,
Master, It was I -
