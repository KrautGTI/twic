I knew that I had gained
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
I knew that I had gained
And yet I knew not how
By Diminution it was not
But Discipline unto
A Rigor unrelieved
Except by the Content
Another bear it's Duplicate
In other Continent.
