A drunkard cannot meet a cork
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A Drunkard cannot meet a Cork
Without a revery,
And so a Fly
This January Day
Jamaicas of remembrance stir,
That send me reeling in -
