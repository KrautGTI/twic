Alone I cannot be
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Alone, I cannot be -
The Hosts - do visit me -
Recordless Company -
Who baffle Key -
They have no Robes, nor Names -
No Almanacs - nor Climes -
But general Homes
Like Gnomes -
Their Coming, may be known
By Couriers within -
Their going - is not -
For they're never gone -
