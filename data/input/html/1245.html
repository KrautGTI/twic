These tested our horizon
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
These tested Our Horizon -
Then disappeared
As Birds before achieving
A Latitude.
Our Retrospection of Them
A fixed Delight,
But Our Anticipation
A Dice - a Doubt -
