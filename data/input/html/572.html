I reason, earth is short
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
I reason, Earth is short -
And Anguish - absolute -
And many hurt,
But, what of that?
I reason, we could die -
The best Vitality
Cannot excel Decay,
But, what of that?
I reason, that in Heaven -
Somehow, it will be even -
Some new Equation, given -
But, what of that?
