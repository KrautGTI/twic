Bind me -- I still can sing --
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
Bind me -- I still can sing --
Banish -- my mandolin
Strikes true within --
Slay -- and my Soul shall rise
Chanting to Paradise --
Still thine.
