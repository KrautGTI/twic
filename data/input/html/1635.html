An honest tear
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
An honest Tear
Is durabler than Bronze -
This Cenotaph
May each that dies -
Reared by itself -
No Deputy suffice -
Gratitude bears
When Obelisk decays
