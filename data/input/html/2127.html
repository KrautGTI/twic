An antiquated tree
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
An Antiquated Tree
Is cherished of the Crow
Because that Junior Foliage is disrespectful now
To venerable Birds
Whose Corporation Coat
Would decorate Oblivion's
Remotest Consulate -
