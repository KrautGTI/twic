Expectation -- is Contentment --
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
Expectation -- is Contentment --
Gain -- Satiety --
But Satiety -- Conviction
Of Nescessity
Of an Austere trait in Pleasure --
Good, without alarm
Is a too established Fortune --
Danger -- deepens Sum --
