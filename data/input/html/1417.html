Of silken speech and specious shoe
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Of Silken Speech, and Specious Shoe -
A Traitor is the Bee -
His Service - to the newest Grace
Present continually -
His Suit, a Chance -
His Troth, a Term
Protracted as the Breeze -
Continual Ban propoundeth He -
Continual Divorce -
