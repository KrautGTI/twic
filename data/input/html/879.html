A secret told
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A Secret told -
Ceases to be a Secret - then -
A Secret - kept -
That - can but One -
Better of it - continual be afraid -
Than it -
And Whom you told it to - beside -
