My friend must be a bird
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
My friend must be a Bird -
Because it flies!
Mortal, my friend must be -
Because it dies!
Barbs has it, like a Bee!
Ah, curious friend!
Thou puzzlest me!
