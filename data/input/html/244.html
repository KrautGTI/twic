By a flower - by a letter
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
By a flower - By a letter
By a nimble love -
If I weld the Rivet faster -
Final fast - above -
Never mind my breathless Anvil!
Never mind Repose!
Never mind the sooty faces
Tugging at the Forge!
