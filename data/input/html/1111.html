A nearness to tremendousness
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A nearness to Tremendousness -
An Agony procures -
Affliction ranges Boundlessness -
Vicinity to Laws
Contentment's quiet Suburb -
Affliction cannot stay
In Acres - It's Location
Is Illocality -
