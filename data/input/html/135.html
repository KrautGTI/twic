"Good night," because we must!
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
"Good night," because we must!
How intricate the Dust!
I would go to know -
Oh Incognito!
Saucy, saucy Seraph,
To elude me so!
Father! they wont tell me!
Wont you tell them to?
