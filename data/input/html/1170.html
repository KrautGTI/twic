If blame be my side - forfeit me
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
If Blame be my side - forfeit Me -
But doom me not to forfeit Thee -
To forfeit Thee? The very name
Is sentence from Belief - and Home -
