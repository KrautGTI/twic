Volcanoes be in Sicily
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Volcanoes be in Sicily
And South America
I judge from my Geography
Volcano nearer here
A Lava step at any time
Am I inclined to climb
A Crater I may contemplate
Vesuvius at Home
