If I should cease to bring a rose
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
If I should cease to bring a Rose
Opon a festal day,
be because the Rose
I have been called away -
If I should cease to take the names
My buds commemorate -
be because finger
Clasps my murmuring lip!
