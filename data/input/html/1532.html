A spider sewed at night
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A Spider sewed at Night
Without a Light
Opon an Arc of White -
If Ruff it was of Dame
Or Shroud of Gnome
Himself himself inform -
Of Immortality
His strategy
Was physiognomy -
