It's little ether hood
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
It's little Ether Hood
Doth sit opon it's Head -
The millinery supple
Of the sagacious God -
Till when it slip away
A nothing at a time -
And Dandelion's Drama
Expires in a stem.
