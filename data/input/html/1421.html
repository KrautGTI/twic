Experiment to me
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Experiment to me is every one I meet
If it contain a Kernel?
The Figure of a nut
Presents opon a Tree
Equally plausibly,
But Meat within, is requisite
To Squirrels, and to Me.
