Oh shadow on the grass!
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Oh Shadow on the Grass -
Art thou a Step or not?
Go make thee fair my Candidate
My nominated Heart -
Oh Shadow on the Grass
While I delay to guess
Some other thou wilt consecrate -
Oh Unelected Face -
