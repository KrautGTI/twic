Ambition cannot find him
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Ambition cannot find him!
Affection does'nt know
How many leagues of Nowhere
Lie between them now!
undistinguished!
Eminent
For our honor,
Immortality!
