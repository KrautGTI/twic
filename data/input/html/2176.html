Now I lay thee down to sleep
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Now I lay thee down to Sleep -
I pray the Lord thy Dust to keep -
And if thou live before thou wake -
I pray the Lord thy Soul to make -
