Not any sunny tone
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Not any sunny tone
From any fervent zone
Find entrance there
Better a grave of Balm
Toward human nature's home
And Robins near
Than a stupendous Tomb
Proclaiming to the gloom
How dead we are -
