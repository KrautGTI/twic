On this wondrous sea
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
On this wondrous sea
Sailing silently,
Ho! Pilot, ho!
Knowest thou the shore
Where no breakers roar -
Where the storm is ?
In the peaceful west
Many the sails at rest -
The anchors fast -
Thither I pilot -
Land Ho! Eternity!
Ashore at last!
