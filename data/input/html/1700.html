"Was not" was all the statement
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
"Was not" - was all the statement -
The unpretension stuns -
Perhaps - the -
They knew no Lexicons -
But lest our speculation
In inanition die
"Because God took him -" tell us -
That was Philology -
