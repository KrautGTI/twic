This dirty little heart
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
This dirty - little - Heart
Is freely mine -
I won it with a Bun -
A Freckled shrine -
But eligibly fair
To him who sees
The Visage of the Soul
And not the knees.
