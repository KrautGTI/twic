No other can reduce
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
No Other can reduce
Our mortal Consequence
Like the remembering it be nought
A Period from hence
But Contemplation for
Cotemporaneous nought
Our single Competition
Jehovah's Estimate.
