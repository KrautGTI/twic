My river runs to thee
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
My River runs to Thee -
Blue Sea - Wilt welcome me?
My River waits reply.
Oh Sea - look graciously!
I'll fetch thee Brooks
From spotted nooks -
Say Sea - take me?
