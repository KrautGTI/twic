The pedigree of honey
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The pedigree of Honey
Does not the Bee,
Nor lineage of Ecstasy
Delay the Butterfly
On spangled journeys to the peak
Of some Thing -
The right of way to Tripoli
A more essential thing -
