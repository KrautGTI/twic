A throe opon the features
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A throe opon the features -
A hurry in the breath -
An of parting
Denominated Death -
An Anguish at the mention
Which when to patience grown,
I've known permission given
To rejoin it's own -
