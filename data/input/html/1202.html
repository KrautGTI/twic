She sped as petals from a rose
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
She sped as Petals from a Rose -
Offended by the Wind
A fleet Aristocrat of Time
Indemnity to find -
Leaving an attitude on Time
Of Monad or of Fly -
But Andes, in the Bosoms where,
It had begun to lie -
