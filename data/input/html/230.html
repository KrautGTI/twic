'Twas such a little - little boat
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
'Twas such a little - little boat
That toddled down the bay!
'Twas such a gallant - gallant sea
That beckoned it away!

That licked it from the Coast -
