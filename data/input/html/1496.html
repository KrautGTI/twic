There is a strength in proving that it can be borne
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
There is a strength in proving that it can be borne
Although it tear -
What are the sinews of such cordage for
Except to bear
The ship might be of satin had it not to fight -
