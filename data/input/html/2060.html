A little overflowing word
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A little overflowing word
That any, hearing, had inferred
For Ardor or for Tears,
Though Generations pass away,
Traditions ripen and decay,
As eloquent appears -
