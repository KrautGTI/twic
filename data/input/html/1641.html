Because my brook is fluent
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Because my Brook is fluent
I know 'tis dry -
Because my Brook is silent
It is the Sea -
And startled at it's swelling
I try to flee
To where the Strong assure me
Is "no more Sea" -
