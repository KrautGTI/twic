The past is such a curious creature
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The Past is such a curious Creature
To look her in the Face
A Transport may receipt us
Or a Disgrace -
Unarmed if any meet her
I charge him fly
Her faded Ammunition
Might yet reply.
