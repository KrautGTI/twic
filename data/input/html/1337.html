Dying at my music!
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Dying at my music!
Bubble! Bubble!
Hold me till the Octave's run!
Quick! Burst the Windows!
Ritardando!
Phials left, and the Sun!
