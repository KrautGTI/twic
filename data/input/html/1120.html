Pain expands the time
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Pain - expands the Time -
Ages coil within
The minute Circumference
Of a single Brain -
Pain contracts - the Time -
Occupied with Shot
Gammuts of Eternities
Are as they were not -
