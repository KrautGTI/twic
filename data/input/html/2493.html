Garlands for Queens, may be --
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
Garlands for Queens, may be --
Laurels -- for rare degree
Of soul or sword.
Ah -- but remembering me --
Ah -- but remembering thee --
Nature in chivalry --
Nature in charity --
Nature in equity --
The Rose ordained!
