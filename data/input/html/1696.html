The sea said "Come" to the brook
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The Sea said Come - to the Brook.
The Brook said "Let me grow!"
The Sea said "Then you will be a Sea" -
"I want a Brook - Come Now!"
