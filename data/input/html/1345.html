The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Not to discover is
The Artifice of strength -
Impregnability inheres
As much through Consciousness
Of faith of others in itself
As Pyramidal Nerve
Behind the most unconscious clock
What Pointers move -
