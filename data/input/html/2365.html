There is a solitude of space
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
There is a solitude of space
A solitude of sea
A solitude of Death, but these
Society shall be
Compared with that profounder site
That polar privacy
A soul admitted to itself -
