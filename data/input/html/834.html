Except the smaller size
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Except the smaller size
No Lives are Round -
These - hurry to a Sphere -
And show - and end -
The Larger - slower grow -
And later hang -
The Summers of Hesperides
Are long -
