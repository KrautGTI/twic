Distrustful of the gentian
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Distrustful of the Gentian -
And just to turn away,
The of her fringes
Chid my perfidy -
Weary for my
I will singing go -
I shall not feel the sleet - then -
I shall not fear the snow.
