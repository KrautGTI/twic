"They have not chosen me" - he said
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
"They have not chosen me," he said,
"But I have chosen them".
Brave - Broken hearted statement -
Uttered in Bethleem!
could not have told it,
But since Jesus -
Sovreign! Know a Daisy
Thy dishonor shared!
