So from the mould
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
So from the mould,
Scarlet and Gold
Many a Bulb will rise -
Hidden away, cunningly,
From sagacious eyes -
So from Cocoon
Many a Worm
Leap so Highland gay,
like me,
Peasants like Thee,
Gaze perplexedly -
