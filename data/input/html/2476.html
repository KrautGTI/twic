Baffled for just a day or two --
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
Baffled for just a day or two --
Embarrassed -- not afraid --
Encounter in my garden
An unexpected Maid.
She beckons, and the woods start --
She nods, and all begin --
Surely, such a country
I was never in!
