Sweet pirate of the heart
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Sweet Pirate of the Heart,
Not Pirate of the Sea -
What wrecketh thee?
Some Spice's Mutiny -
Some Attar's perfidy?
Confide in me -
