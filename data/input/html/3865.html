Bees are Black, with Gilt Surcingles --
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Johnson, 1955
Bees are Black, with Gilt Surcingles --
Bucaneers of Buzz.
Ride abroad in ostentation
And subsist on Fuzz.
Fuzz ordained -- not Fuzz contingent --
Marrows of the Hill.
Jugs -- a Universe's fracture
Could not jar or spill.
