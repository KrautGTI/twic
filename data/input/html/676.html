You constituted time
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
You constituted Time -
I deemed Eternity
A Revelation of Yourself -
'Twas therefore Deity
The Absolute - removed
The Relative away -
That I unto Himself adjust
My slow idolatry -
