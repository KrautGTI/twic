How far is it to heaven?
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
How far is it to Heaven?
As far as Death this way -
Of River or of Ridge beyond
Was no discovery.
How far is it to Hell?
As far as Death this way -
How far left hand the Sepulchre
Defies Topography.
