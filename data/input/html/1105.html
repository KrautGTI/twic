The only news I know
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The only news I know
Is Bulletins all Day
From Immortality.
The only Shows I see -
Tomorrow and Today -
Perchance Eternity -
The only one I meet
Is God - The only Street -
Existence - This traversed
If other news there be -
Or admirabler show -
I'll tell it You -
