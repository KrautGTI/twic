After all birds have been investigated
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
First at the March -
Competing with the Wind
Her joyful Cry
Exalts us like a friend
Last to adhere
When summers swerve away
Fortitude - stanched with melody
