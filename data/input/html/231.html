"Sown in dishonor"!
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
"Sown in dishonor"?
Ah, indeed!
May dishonor be?
If I were so fine, myself,
I'd notice nobody!
"Sown in Corruption"?
By no means!
Apostle is askew!
Corinthians 1. 15. narrates
A circumstance - or two!
