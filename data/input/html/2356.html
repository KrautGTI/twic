Endanger it, and the demand
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Endanger it, and the Demand
Of tickets for a sigh
Amazes the Humility
Of Credibility -
Recover it to nature
And that dejected Fleet
Find Consternation's carnival
Divested of it's meat
