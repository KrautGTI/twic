It is dead - find it
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
It is dead - Find it -
Out of sound - Out of Sight -
"Happy"? Which is wiser -
You, or the Wind?
"Conscious"? Wont you ask that -
Of the low Ground?
"Homesick"? Many met it -
Even through them - This cannot testify -
Themself - as dumb -
