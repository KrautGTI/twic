To love thee year by year
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
To love thee Year by Year -
May less appear
Than sacrifice, and cease -
However, dear,
Forever might be short, I thought to show -
And so I pieced it, with a flower, now.
