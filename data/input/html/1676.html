Until the desert knows
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Until the Desert knows
That Water grows
His Sands suffice
But let him once suspect
That Caspian Fact
Sahara dies
Utmost is relative -
Have not or Have
Adjacent Sums
Enough - the first Abode
On the familiar Road
Galloped in Dreams -
