One and one are one
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
One and One - are One -
Two - be finished using -
Well enough for schools -
But for minor Choosing -
Life - just - Or Death -
Or the Everlasting -
More - would be too vast
For the Soul's Comprising -
