Me change! Me alter!
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Me, change! Me, alter!
Then I will, when on the Everlasting Hill
A Smaller Purple grows -
At Sunset, or a lesser glow
Flickers opon Cordillera -
At Day's superior close!
