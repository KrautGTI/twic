I had the glory - that will do
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
I had the Glory - that will do -
An Honor, Thought can turn her to
When lesser Fames invite -
With one long "Nay" -
Bliss' early shape
Deforming - Dwindling - Gulphing up -
Time's possibility -
