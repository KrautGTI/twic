If it had no pencil
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
If it had no pencil,
Would it try mine -
Worn - now - and - sweet,
Writing much to thee.
If it had no word -
Would it make the Daisy,
Most as big as I was -
When it plucked me?
