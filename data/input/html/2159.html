Echo has no magistrate
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Echo has no Magistrate -
Catch a Drop of Dew
And the Sun will free it
With a sneer at you -
Follow fine Orion till you furl your Eye -
Dazzlingly decamping
He is still more high -
