I keep my pledge
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
I keep my pledge.
I was not called -
Death did not notice me.
I bring my Rose -
I plight again -
By every sainted Bee -
By Daisy called from hillside -
By Bobolink from lane -
Blossom and I -
Her oath, and mine -
Will surely come again -
