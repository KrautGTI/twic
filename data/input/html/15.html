Garlands for queens may be
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Garlands for Queens, may be -
Laurels - for rare degree
Of soul or sword -
Ah - but remembering me -
Ah - but remembering thee -
Nature in chivalry -
Nature in charity -
Nature in equity -
The Rose ordained!
