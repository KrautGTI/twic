Not seeing, still we know
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Not seeing, still we know -
Not knowing, guess -
Not guessing, smile and hide
And half caress -
And quake - and turn away,
Seraphic fear -
Is Eden's innuendo
"If you dare"?
