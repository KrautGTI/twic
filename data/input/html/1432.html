The opening and the close
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The Opening and the Close
Of Being, are alike
Or differ, if they do,
As Bloom opon a Stalk -
That from an equal Seed
Unto an equal Bud
Go parallel, perfected
In that they have decayed -
