The butterfly's assumption gown
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The Butterfly's Assumption Gown
In chrysoprase apartment hung
Unrolls the Sun
How condescending to descend
And be of the friend
In a town!
