Unable are the loved to die
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Unable are the Loved to die
For Love is Immortality,
Nay, it is Deity -
Unable they that love - to die
For Love reforms Vitality
Into Divinity.
