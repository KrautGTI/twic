God permits industrious angels
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
God permits industrious Angels -
Afternoons - to play -
I met one - forgot my schoolmates -
All - for Him - straightway -
God calls home - the Angels - promptly -
At the Setting Sun -
I missed mine - how -
After playing
