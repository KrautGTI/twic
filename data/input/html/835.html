Except the smaller size
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Except the smaller size
No lives are round -
These - hurry to a sphere
And show and end -
The larger - slower grow
And later hang -
The Summers of Hesperides
Are long.
