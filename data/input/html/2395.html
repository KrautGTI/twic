The right to perish might be thought
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The right to perish might be thought
An undisputed right
Attempt it, and the Universe
Upon the opposite
Will concentrate it's officers -
You cannot even die
But nature and mankind must pause
To pay you scrutiny -
