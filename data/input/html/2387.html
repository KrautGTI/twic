Rather arid delight
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Rather arid delight
If Contentment accrue
Make an abstemious ecstasy
Not so good as joy -
But Rapture's Expense
Must not be incurred
With a knocking
And the Rent unpaid -
