The long sigh of the frog
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The long sigh of the Frog
Opon a Summer's Day
Enacts intoxication
Opon the Passer by.
But his receding Swell
Substantiates a Peace
That makes the Ear inordinate
For corporal release -
