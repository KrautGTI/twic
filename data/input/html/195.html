Our lives are Swiss
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Our lives are Swiss -
So still - so Cool -
Till some odd afternoon
The Alps neglect their Curtains
And we look farther on!
Italy stands the other side!
While like a guard between -
The solemn Alps -
The siren Alps
Forever intervene!
