Besides this May
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Besides this May
We know
There is Another -
How fair
Our speculations of the Foreigner!
Some know Him whom We knew -
Sweet Wonder -
A Nature be
Where Saints, and our plain going Neighbor
Keep May!
