I had no time to hate
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
I had no time to Hate -
Because
The Grave would hinder me -
And Life was not so
Ample I
Could finish - Enmity -
Nor had I time to Love -
But since
Some Industry must be -
The little Toil of Love -
I thought
Be large enough for Me -
