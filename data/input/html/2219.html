To be forgot by thee
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
To be forgot by thee
Surpasses Memory
Of other minds
The Heart cannot forget
Unless it contemplate
What it declines
I was regarded then
Raised from oblivion
A single time
To be remembered what -
Worthy to be forgot
My low renown
