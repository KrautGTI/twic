If I could tell how glad I was
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
If I could tell how glad I was
I should not be so glad -
But when I cannot make the Force
Nor mould it into word
I know it is a sign
That new Dilemma be
From mathematics further off
Than from Eternity
