Reportless subjects, to the quick
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Reportless Subjects, to the Quick
Continual addressed -
But foreign as the Dialect
Of Danes, unto the rest.
Reportless Measures, to the Ear
Susceptive - stimulus -
But like an Oriental Tale
To others, fabulous -
