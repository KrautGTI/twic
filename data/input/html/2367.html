Lightly stepped a yellow star
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Lightly stepped a yellow star
To it's lofty place
Loosed the Moon her silver hat
From her lustral Face
All of evening softly lit
As an Astral Hall
Father I observed to Heaven
