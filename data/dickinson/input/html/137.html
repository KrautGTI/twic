South winds jostle them
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
South winds jostle them -
Bumblebees come -
Hover - hesitate -
Drink - and are gone -
Butterflies pause
On their passage Cashmere -
I - softly plucking
Present them here -
