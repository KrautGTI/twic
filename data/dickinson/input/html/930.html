Only God detect the sorrow
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Only God - detect the Sorrow -
Only God -
The Jehovahs - are no Babblers -
Unto God -
God the Son - confide it -
Still secure -
God the Spirit's Honor -
Just as sure -
