Sweet - you forgot - but I remembered
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Just to be Rich -
To waste my Guinea
On so Broad a Heart -
Just to be Poor -
For Barefoot Pleasure
You - Sir - Shut me out -
