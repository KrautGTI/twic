A long - long sleep
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A long - long Sleep -
A famous - Sleep -
That makes no show for Morn -
By Stretch of Limb - or stir of Lid -
An independent One -
Was ever idleness like This?
Opon a Bank of Ston
To bask the Centuries away -
Nor once look up - for Noon?
