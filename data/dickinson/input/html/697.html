We'll pass without a parting
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
We'll pass without a parting -
So to spare
Certificate of absence -
Deeming where
I left Her, I could find Her
If I tried -
This way - I keep from missing
Those who died
