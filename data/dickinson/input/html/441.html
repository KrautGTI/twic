Did we disobey him?
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Did we disobey Him?
Just one time!
Charged us to forget Him -
But we could'nt learn!
Were Himself - such a Dunce -
What would we - do?
Love the dull lad - best -
Oh, would'nt you?
