Must be a wo
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Must be a Wo -
A loss or so -
To bend the eye
Best Beauty's way -
But - once aslant
It notes Delight
As difficult
As Stalactite -
A Common Bliss
Were had for less -
The price - is
Even as the Grace -
Our Lord - thought no
Extravagance
To pay - a Cross -
