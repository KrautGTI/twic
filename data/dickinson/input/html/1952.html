I have no life to live
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
I have no life to live
But lead it here
Nor any Death but lest
Abased from there -
Nor Plea for World to come
Nor Wisdoms new
Except through this extent
The loving you -
