A sepal - petal - and a thorn
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A sepal - petal - and a thorn
Opon a common summer's morn -
A flask of Dew - A Bee or two -
A Breeze - a'caper in the trees -
And I'm a Rose!
