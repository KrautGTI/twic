Truth is as old as God
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Truth - is as old as God -
His Twin Identity -
And will endure as long as He -
A Co Eternity -
And perish, on the Day
That He is borne away
From Mansion of the Universe -
A lifeless Deity -
