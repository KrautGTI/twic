Touch lightly nature's sweet guitar
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Touch lightly Nature's sweet Guitar
Unless thou know'st the Tune
Or every Bird will point at thee
Because a Bard too soon -
