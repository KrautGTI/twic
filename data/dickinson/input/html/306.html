Come slowly - Eden!
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Come slowly - Eden!
Lips unused to Thee -
Bashful - sip thy Jessamines -
As the fainting Bee -
Reaching late his flower,
Round her chamber hums -
Counts his nectars -
Enters - and is lost in Balms.
