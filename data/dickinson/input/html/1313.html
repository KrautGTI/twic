Satisfaction is the agent
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Satisfaction - is the Agent
Of Satiety -
Want - a quiet Comissary
For Infinity -
To possess, is past the instant
We achieve the Joy -
Immortality contented
Were Anomaly -
