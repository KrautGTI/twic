A death blow is a life blow to some
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
A Death blow - is a Life blow - to Some -
Who, till they died,
Did not alive - become -
Who had they lived
Had died, but when
They died, Vitality begun -
