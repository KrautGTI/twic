For death - or rather
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
For Death - or rather
For the Things 'twould buy -
This - put away
Life's Opportunity -
The Things that Death will buy
Are Room -
Escape from Circumstances -
And a Name -
With Gifts of Life
How Death's Gifts may compare -
We know not -
For the Rates - lie Here -
