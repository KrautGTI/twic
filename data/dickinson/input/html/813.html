The soul that hath a guest
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The Soul that hath a Guest
Doth seldom go abroad -
Diviner Crowd at Home -
Obliterate the need -
And Courtesy forbid
A Host's departure when
Opon Himself be visiting
The Emperor of Men -
