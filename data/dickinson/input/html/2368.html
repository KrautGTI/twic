Peril as a possession
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Peril as a Possession
'Tis good to bear
Danger disintegrates satiety
There's Basis there -
Begets an awe
That searches Human Nature's creases
As clean as Fire
