Oh shadow on the grass!
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Oh Shadow on the Grass!
Art thou a step or not?
Go make thee fair, my Candidate -
My nominated Heart!
Oh Shadow on the Grass!
While I delayed to dress
Some other thou did'st consecrate -
Oh unelected Face!
