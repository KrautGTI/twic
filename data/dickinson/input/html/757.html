Conjecturing a climate
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Conjecturing a Climate
Of unsuspended Suns -
Adds poignancy to Winter -
The shivering Fancy turns
To a fictitious Country
To palliate a Cold -
Not obviated of Degree -
Nor eased - of Latitude -
