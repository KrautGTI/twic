"Lethe" in my flower
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
"Lethe" in my flower,
Of which they who drink,
In the fadeless Orchards
Hear the bobolink!
Merely flake or petal
As the Eye beholds
Jupiter! my father!
I perceive the rose!
