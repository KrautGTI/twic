All that I do
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
All that I do
Is in review
To his enamored mind
I know his eye
Where e'er I ply
Is pushing close behind
Not any Port
Not any flight
But he doth there preside
What omnipresence lies in wait
For her to be a Bride
