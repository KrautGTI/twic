The rat is the concisest tenant
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The rat is the concisest tenant
He pays no rent
Repudiates the obligation
On schemes intent -
Balking our wit
To sound or circumvent
Hate cannot harm
A Foe so reticent
Or decree prohibit him
Lawful as Equilibrium -
