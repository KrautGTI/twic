That it will never come again
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
That it will never come again
Is what makes life so sweet.
Believing what we dont believe
Does not exhilirate .
That if it be, it be at best
An ablative estate -
This instigates an appetite
Precisely opposite.
