The way hope builds his house
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The way Hope builds his House
It is not with a sill -
Nor Rafter - has that Edifice
But only Pinnacle -
Abode in as supreme
This superficies
As if it were of Ledges smit
Or mortised with the Laws -
