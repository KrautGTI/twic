How destitute is he
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
How destitute is he
Whose Gold is firm -
Who finds it every time
The same stale Sum -
When Love with but a Pence
Will so display
As he had no esteem
For India -
