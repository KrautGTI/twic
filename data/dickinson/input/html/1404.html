Ribbons of the year
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Ribbons of the Year -
Multitude Brocade -
Worn to Nature's Party once
Then, as flung aside
As a faded Bead
Or a Wrinkled Pearl -
Who shall charge the Vanity
Of the Maker's Girl?
