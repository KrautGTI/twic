To flee from memory
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
To flee from memory
Had we the Wings
Many would fly
Inured to slower things
Birds with dismay
Would scan the mighty Van
Of men escaping
From the mind of man
