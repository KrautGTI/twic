All I may if small
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
All I may - if small,
Do it not display
Larger, for it's Totalness?
'Tis Economy
To bestow a World
And withold a Star,
Utmost - is munificence -
Less - tho' Larger, Poor -
