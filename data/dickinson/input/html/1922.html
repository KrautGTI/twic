Trusty as the stars
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Trusty as the stars
Who quit their shining working
Prompt as when I lit them
In Genesis' new house,
Durable as dawn
Whose antiquated blossom
Makes a world's suspense
Perish and rejoice.
