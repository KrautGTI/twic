An antiquated tree
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
An Antiquated Tree
Is cherished by the Crow.
Because that Junior Foliage
Is disrespectful now
To venerable Birds
Whose Corporation Coat
Would decorate Oblivion's
Fantastic Consulate -
