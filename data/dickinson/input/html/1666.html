Fortitude incarnate
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Fortitude incarnate
Here is laid away
In the swift Partitions
Of the awful Sea -
Babble of the Happy
Cavil of the Bold
Hoary the Fruition
But the Deep is old
Architect of Ocean
Thy tumultuous Rooms
Suit me at a venture
Better than the Tombs
