The mind lives on the heart
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The Mind lives on the Heart
Like any Parasite -
If that be full of meat
The mind is fat -
But if the Heart be lean
The boldest mind will pine
Throw not to the divine
Like Dog a Bone
