We learn in the retreating
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
We learn in the Retreating
How vast an one
Was recently among us -
A Perished Sun
Endear in the departure
How doubly more
Than all the Golden presence
It was - before -
