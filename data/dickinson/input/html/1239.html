The poets light but lamps
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
The Poets light but Lamps -
Themselves - go out -
The Wicks they stimulate
If vital Light
Inhere as do the Suns -
Each Age a Lens
Disseminating their
Circumference -
