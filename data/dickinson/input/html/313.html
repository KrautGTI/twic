If I should'nt be alive
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
If I should'nt be alive
When the Robins come,
Give the one in Red Cravat,
A Memorial crumb -
If I could'nt thank you,
Being fast asleep,
You will know I'm trying
With my Granite lip!
