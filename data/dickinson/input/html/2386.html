Guest am I to have
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Guest am I to have
Light my northern room
Why to cordiality so averse to come
Other friends adjourn
Other bonds decay
Why avoid so narrowly
My fidelity -
