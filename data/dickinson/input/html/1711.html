Could hope inspect her basis
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Could Hope inspect her Basis
Her Craft were done -
Has a fictitious Charter
Or it has none -
Balked in the vastest instance
But to renew -
Felled by but one assassin -
Prosperity -
