Glory is that bright tragic thing
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Glory is that bright tragic thing
That for an instant
Means Dominion
Warms some poor name
That never felt the Sun
Gently replacing
In oblivion -
