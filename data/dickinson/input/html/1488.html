After the sun comes out
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
After the Sun comes out
How it alters the World -
Wagons like messengers hurry about
Yesterday is old -
All men meet as if
Each foreclosed a news -
Fresh as a Cargo from Balize
Nature's qualities -
