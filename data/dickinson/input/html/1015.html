No bobolink reverse his singing
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
No Bobolink - reverse His Singing
When the only Tree
Ever He minded occupying
By the Farmer be -
Clove to the Root -
His Spacious Future -
Best Horizon - gone -
Brave Bobolink -
Whose Music be His
Only Anodyne -
