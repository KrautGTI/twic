Pompless no life can pass away
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Pompless no Life can pass away -
The lowliest career
To the same pageant wends it's way
As that exalted here -
How cordial is the mystery
The Hospitable Pall -
A this way beckons spaciously
A miracle for all -
