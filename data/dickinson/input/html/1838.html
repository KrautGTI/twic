Opon a lilac sea
The Poems of Emily Dickinson, Variorum Edition, Franklin, 1998
Opon a Lilac Sea
To toss incessantly
His Plush Alarm
Who fleeing from the Spring
The Spring avenging fling
To Dooms of Balm -
